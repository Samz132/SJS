WELCOME TO SJS CANADA

SJS Canada is a non-profit organization dedicated to spreading awareness about the extremely rare life-threatening medical illness. Our founder and president Sonia W. Croasdaile, unfortunately was stricken with Stevens Johnson Syndrome and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis in 2011. Her miraculous survival inspired her to create a well needed outlet to spread awareness about this life threatening medical illness.
